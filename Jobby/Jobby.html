About the Company

We created an app to help college seniors find the right job after graduating from school. We want to streamline the application process and reduce the stress. Tired of wasting time filling out tens of applications only to not land an interview? That’s where Prospira comes in, with our tools you will be landing more interviews, getting more jobs, and finding the right career for you.

Talking to our peers, we understood how stressful and time consuming getting a job can be. So we created an app to help remove stress and save users time throughout the job application process. With our app users can build their resume, get interviews and career coaching, and use our custom job matching tool to find the right fit for them. We are committed to our customers' success, and think the job application process can be way too confusing at times.
