Our Story

In our own job search we have encountered every type of failure. From no response at all to getting to an interview only to realize that the job is not the right fit for you. After constant failure we sought out to find a better way to find a job. After long hours of searching we decided to create our own app that's built the right way in order to connect you with the right job that is the best fit for you.
